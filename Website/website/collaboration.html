Collaboration


Aside from the forum, users can come together on this page to collaborate and contribute to innovative new ways of reducing emissions and creating awesome strategies to solve complex environmental problems that Christchurch will face in the future.

We intend on having many collaborative software features in this section, with a main focus on:

  • Anonymous and private survery systems via online polling softwares such as Jotform, SurveyLegend, and DialogLoop.
  • Contributive GitHub projects.
  • Personal situations, responses, and active team-based solutions.

As caretakes of Aotearoa New Zealand, we care deeply about the land and its relationship to environmental change, and our activate participation within such a process; we are committed to hearing all voices and to educating future generations so that positive change may be generated.

We also contributed to open-source collaboration using software that enables such practices; in the future, we intend on founding further interactions within the open-source community sphere to better foster national and international relations.
